# Dark Fantasy Ink

## Style Identity

黒インクと深い緑を基調にしたダークファンタジー挿絵風。
重厚さは出すが、視認性を保ち、ノベル素材として使える余白を残す。

## Global Prompt Block

```text
Style: dark fantasy ink illustration, black ink linework, deep green shadows, etched texture, moody atmosphere, elegant gothic detail, controlled contrast, illustrated visual novel asset style.
Color: near-black, deep emerald, muted bone white, dim gold accents, low saturation.
Lighting: dramatic low-key lighting, rim light only where needed, no bright neon glow.
Composition: strong silhouette, controlled negative space, readable focal point, cinematic but not cluttered.
Avoid: cute pastel mood, glossy 3D, photorealistic horror gore, excessive noise, illegible detail, random text, watermark, inconsistent line weight.
```

## Character

```text
Create a character asset in the shared dark fantasy ink style.
Use a strong silhouette, controlled ink linework, restrained gothic detail, and deep green shadow accents.
Keep the face and posture readable for narrative use.
No gore, no random symbols, no background unless requested.
```

## Background

```text
Create a background in the shared dark fantasy ink style.
Use atmospheric depth, deep green-black shadows, elegant architectural or natural forms, and clear negative space for dialogue UI.
No characters unless requested.
```

## Item

```text
Create an item asset in the shared dark fantasy ink style.
Use a readable silhouette, dark ink contour, deep green shading, and a small amount of bone white or dim gold highlight.
Keep it iconic and not overly detailed.
```

## Title Logo

```text
Create a title logo direction in the shared dark fantasy ink style.
Use sharp but readable lettering, ink-like edges, deep green shadowing, and subtle dim gold or bone white accents.
Keep text exact when provided.
```

## Title Background

```text
Create a title background in the shared dark fantasy ink style.
Use a symbolic dark scene, controlled contrast, moody green-black atmosphere, and clean space for the title logo.
Avoid clutter and unreadable high-detail texture.
```

## Style Checklist

- 黒と深緑が主役になっているか
- 線の密度が素材間で揃っているか
- 暗いが読みやすいか
- 余白があり、ノベルUIを載せられるか
- 怖さよりも物語性と美しさが優先されているか
